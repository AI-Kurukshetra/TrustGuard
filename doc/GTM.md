# Go-To-Market Plan

## ICP (Initial Customer Profile)

- Mid-market e-commerce businesses with active chargeback pain.
- Fintech startups with fast user and transaction growth.
- Teams requiring API-first integration and analyst workflows.

## Positioning

- "Fast to integrate fraud protection with explainable scoring and analyst-ready workflows."
- Emphasize low integration effort, transparent pricing, and operational visibility.

## Initial Channels

- Direct outbound to risk/compliance leaders at target ICP accounts.
- Partnerships with payment processors and commerce platforms.
- Technical content-led acquisition (API guides, integration playbooks, benchmark reports).

## Regional Priority

- Start where digital payment adoption is high and regulatory pressure is strong.
- Expand regionally after baseline policy/compliance templates are validated.

## Launch Motions

1. Design partner cohort (5-10 merchants).
2. Short implementation cycle with API-first onboarding.
3. Publish quantified outcomes (false-positive reduction, latency, fraud prevented).
4. Convert to repeatable sales motion using case studies and reference architectures.

## Core GTM Metrics

- Time to first protected transaction.
- Trial-to-paid conversion.
- Fraud loss reduction per merchant.
- Gross retention and expansion (add-ons / higher tiers).
