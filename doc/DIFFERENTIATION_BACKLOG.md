# Differentiation Backlog

## Goal

Prioritize capabilities that separate TrustGuard from established vendors while preserving MVP delivery speed.

## Near-Term (Post-MVP)

1. Explainable decision artifacts in analyst views and API responses.
2. Dynamic risk-threshold tuning by merchant behavior and feedback loops.
3. Advanced graph-based fraud ring scoring and linked-entity investigations.
4. Chargeback propensity modeling with proactive prevention playbooks.

## Mid-Term

1. Cross-merchant intelligence with privacy-preserving aggregation.
2. Automated model evaluation and drift alerts.
3. Multi-channel detection parity (web, mobile, API-originated events).
4. Fraud simulation sandbox for controlled scenario testing.

## Long-Term

1. Federated learning controls across tenant-isolated model cohorts.
2. Synthetic fraud generation pipelines for safer training datasets.
3. Privacy-preserving analytics patterns (encrypted features, constrained sharing).

## Prioritization Rubric

- Business impact: expected fraud-loss reduction and conversion impact.
- Delivery effort: engineering + data + operations complexity.
- Defensibility: difficulty for competitors to replicate quickly.
- Adoption readiness: how quickly customers can operationalize the capability.
